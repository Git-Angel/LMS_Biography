About Myself

My name is Angelina Onuoha, from a small town in imo state, Nigeria.
I was born and brought up in Lagos state in a town that never sleeps.
Growing up amongst many siblings in a space where you cannot have all you
want, is quite a "bittersweet" experience now that I think about it.This
reflects the complexity of emotions tied to both loss and gain.What I do
now isn't what I grew up wanting to do, but I am glad I found Tech!

Since I came into contact with technology and understood its potential for
automation and problem-solving, I have been eager to deepen my knowledge and
explore its vast possibilities. Witnessing how technology can streamline processes
and improve lives sparked a passion within me to become part of this ever-evolving field.

Why I joined ALT school Africa.

joined the ALT School of Engineering program because I believe it provides the
perfect opportunity for me to acquire the advanced skills and knowledge necessary
to turn my enthusiasm for technology into meaningful contributions. The program's
emphasis on practical applications and innovation will help me envision creative
solutions that will address real-world challenges.

I am excited to collaborate with like-minded peers to achieve this this.

My goals for the Program

During my time in the School of Engineering, I aim to achieve the following:

Build a strong foundation in engineering principles, tools, and methodologies to
solve complex problems effectively.

Participate in practical, team-based projects that simulate real-world challenges,
allowing me to apply theoretical knowledge and refine my technical skills.

Explore emerging technologies and contribute to research initiatives, particularly
in areas like renewable energy, automation, or sustainable design.

Work with peers from diverse backgrounds to tackle interdisciplinary challenges,
preparing me for the collaborative nature of engineering in industry settings.

Connect with mentors, industry leaders, and fellow students to create relationships
that will support my career growth and inspire continuous learning.

Graduate from the program equipped with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to
pursue a successful career as an engineer and make a positive impact on society.
